Magic Morning - Your Day, Your Way

Welcome to Magic Morning

Your perfect start to the day. Powered by Horizons.

Highlights of the app include:

  • Customized news report based on your positivity preferences.
  • Your choice of receiving a horoscope, affirmation, and/or spiritual reading.
  • A motivating quip from your favorite personal development influencer.
  • Up-to-the-minute weather report based on your location.

What problem is your project solving?

The "Magic Morning" app is aimed at solving the problem of a disorganized and disoriented start to the day.
